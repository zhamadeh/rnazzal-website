Projects

A selection of ongoing and completed bodies of work.

Vibrations from Gaza (2024)

Short documentary that centres Deaf children in Gaza who perceive bombardment through vibration rather than sound. The work foregrounds touch, tremor, and the constant presence of drones to rethink how violence is sensed.

Driving in Palestine (2010–2020; book 2023)

Long-form photographic study produced from inside moving vehicles across the West Bank. The series records checkpoints, watchtowers, walls, signage, and small gestures of daily life, assembling a road atlas of constraint and endurance.

We, the Wild Plants and Fruit Trees (2022– )

Photographs and notes tracing the lives of wild flora and cultivated trees within militarized ecologies—botany as witness to land use, enclosure, and care.

Invisible (2014)

Video and photographic installation engaging with incarceration and political prisoners; hundreds of small images and several channels of video form a field of fragments and testimony.
