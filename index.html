Rehab Nazzal

Multidisciplinary artist working with photography, video, sound, and installation to examine surveillance, mobility, vibration, and ecologies of care across Palestine.

Contact: renazzal@gmail.com · Represented by Monica Reyes Gallery

Recent work

Vibrations from Gaza (2024)

Short documentary centring Deaf children in Gaza who sense bombardment through vibration rather than sound.

Driving in Palestine (2010–2020; book 2023)

Black‑and‑white photographs from moving vehicles—an atlas of surveillance, constraint, and everyday life across the West Bank.

Dead Sea Study

Shoreline photographs tracing extraction, subsidence, and environmental transformation.

We, the Wild Plants and Fruit Trees (2022– )

Photographs and field notes following wild flora and cultivated trees as witnesses to enclosure and care.
