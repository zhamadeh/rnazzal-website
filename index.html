Rehab Nazzal

Multidisciplinary artist working with photography, video, sound, and installation to examine surveillance, mobility, vibration, and ecologies of care across Palestine.

Contact: renazzal@gmail.com · Represented by Monica Reyes Gallery

Featured project

Vibrations from Gaza (2024)

A short film created with Deaf children and families in Gaza. Rather than audible explosions, the work centres vibration as a primary sense—how war registers through the body when sound is inaccessible or unsafe.

Series

Driving in Palestine (2010–2020; book 2023)

Photographs from moving vehicles trace watchtowers, walls, and checkpoints in the West Bank—mapping architectures of control alongside everyday movement.

Dead Sea Study

Shoreline photographs tracing extraction, sinkholes, and environmental change.

We, the Wild Plants and Fruit Trees (2022– )

Photographs and field notes following wild flora and cultivated trees as living records of enclosure and care.
