Driving in Palestine / التحرّك في فلسطين (Fernwood, 2023)

A research‑creation project of 160 black‑and‑white photographs captured from moving vehicles across the West Bank (2010–2020), with hand‑drawn maps and essays in English and Arabic. The book documents the politics of surveillance and mobility and the architectures that constrain Palestinian movement.
