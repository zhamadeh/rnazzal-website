About

Rehab Nazzal is a Palestinian-born multidisciplinary artist whose work examines mobility, vibration, incarceration, and ecologies of care in Palestine.

Working across photography, video, sound, and installation, she explores how structures of control shape sensing and everyday movement. Her work has been exhibited nationally and internationally, sparking public conversations about censorship and representation.
