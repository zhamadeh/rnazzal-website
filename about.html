About

Rehab Nazzal is a Palestinian-born artist and educator based between Canada and Palestine. Working across photography, video, sound, and installation, she investigates how structures of occupation shape sensing, movement, and ecology.

Education & Teaching

  • PhD, Art & Visual Culture — research on colonial violence, witnessing, and experimental documentary practices
  • MFA — lens-based media and installation
  • Teaching and talks across universities and galleries; community-based workshops and presentations
