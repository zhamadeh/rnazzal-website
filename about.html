About

Rehab Nazzal is a Palestinian-born multidisciplinary artist whose work examines mobility, vibration, incarceration, and ecologies of care in Palestine.

Working across photography, video, sound, and installation, she explores how structures of control shape sensing and everyday movement. Her projects have been shown in museums and galleries across Canada and internationally.

Teaching: Dar Al‑Kalima University (Bethlehem), Simon Fraser University, Western University, Ottawa School of Art. Awards include SSHRC Doctoral Award and the Documentary Photography for Social Justice Award.
